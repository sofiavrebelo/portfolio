About Me

Hello! My name is Sofia Rebelo, I’m a graphic designer and a (soon to be) front-end developer, from Portugal.

Since the beginning of my career journey as a freelance designer, I felt I was lacking the skills that could bring me to the next level. My interest in programming, lead me to the amazing Front-end Foxes School where I’m learning the basics of web development with JavaScript, HTML, CSS and GitHub.

I value simple content structure, beautiful layouts, intuitive and accessible design and enjoy bringing ideas to life in the browser.
